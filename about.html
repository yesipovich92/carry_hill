Hello and a warm welcome to our Junior School. We are a large, inclusive and welcoming school with 400 pupils aged 7–11 years. Our overriding ethos is to provide excellent education for all pupils regardless of their starting points with high expectations for all within a strong values based culture.

Children enjoy taking part in the wide range of clubs that we have here at our school. We have exciting sports clubs such as Badminton, Cross Country, Football, Gymnastics, Netball, and Rugby.

We achieve high standards and are constantly striving to improve our teaching and learning. We offer an engaging, creative and challenging curriculum–our aim is to engage and inspire children in their learning using a wide range of approaches. We also offer a variety of other extra-curricular opportunities; including outdoor and adventurous residential activities in every year group. The majority of our children begin their school life at our infant school. Our aim is to continue this excellent start and to build upon children’s skills and knowledge, fully preparing them for the challenges of secondary school and later life.
